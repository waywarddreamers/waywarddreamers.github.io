Dreams for the future

The Dream Jar

✦ ✦ ✦

Nothing here is a promise. Everything here is a dream and dreams need people to become real. The more of us there are, the more of this we get to build together.

☀︎ Creative Picnics

Somewhere green, something to make, music in the open air. Bring your work, bring a blanket, bring whoever needs a good afternoon.

✦ Critique Nights

Bring something you want eyes on. We offer thoughts with care, not competition the goal is always to help the work become more itself.

⇄ Skill & Resource Swaps

Teach what you know, learn what you don't. Trade supplies, techniques, references the good stuff that usually stays in one person's notes.

𓍊𓋼𓍊𓋼𓍊 Foraging & Hikes

Wandering somewhere with no agenda except to see what the earth left out for us. Materials, inspiration, or just the walk itself.

◎ Portfolio Nights

Organize, refine, figure out what to show and how to show it together. No one should have to sort through their own work alone.

♔ Themed Potlucks

Creatively themed, communally fed. We haven't decided what comes first but we know it will be delicious and probably a little strange.

The Echo Threads

✦ ✦ ✦

Sometimes you need a place to start. The Echo Threads are optional creative prompts open invitations, not assignments. Bring your response whenever it's ready. There is no late here.

Echo Thread № 001 Always Open
Introduce yourself using your preferred creative medium.

Doesn't matter what form it takes a painting, a poem, a song, a sculpture, a short film, a collage. Just let it say: this is me, and this is how I make things. Bring it whenever you're ready, whether that's your first meetup or your fifteenth.

∞

Echo Threads never close. Missed a meeting, missed a month, missed a year the thread is still open. New members can work on every prompt that's ever been posted, and old ones are always welcome to revisit. We're dreamers. It's what we do.
